Building a Global Brain

About

People assume creativity is a crazy outburst of original ideas. Actually, it is mixing and matching ideas you already know in a different context. When your brain creates a link between what you already know and a new context, that is an original insight.

Now imagine if you are able to map out links for all of your information, and see the links that your friends, family and strangers create. How many more ideas and insights you would have?

That's what Project Meta is about. You create links between information, and so will everyone around you. In doing so, you have a network of ideas from all contexts, domains and backgrounds. This helps you know more, learn more and create more.
